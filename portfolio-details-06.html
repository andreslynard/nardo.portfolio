work : details

the-people-of-taiwan.bnw.01

Just the people of Taiwan, captured in black and white. This project showcases the diverse faces and stories of the Taiwanese people, highlighting their culture and daily lives through a series of striking monochrome photographs.

Sonder, the realization that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own. This project captures the essence of sonder through the lens of black and white photography, focusing on the unique stories and emotions of the Taiwanese.
